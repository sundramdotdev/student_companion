# Features Breakdown

Student Companion offers a robust suite of offline academic tools. Here is a detailed breakdown of what the application can do:

### 1. Attendance Management
- Add subjects with dynamic minimum attendance requirements.
- Tap to mark present, absent, or cancelled.
- **Bunk Calculator:** The app instantly calculates exactly how many classes you can skip without falling below your target percentage, or how many you must attend to recover it.
- View attendance history logs.

### 2. Timetable & Planner
- Add weekly recurring schedules for subjects.
- View daily agendas.
- Automatically links to your attendance tracking subjects.

### 3. GPA & Grades
- **Custom Grading Scales:** Create scales tailored to your university (e.g., 10-point scale, 4-point scale).
- Support for complex Grade point to Letter mappings.
- Create Semesters, add courses with credits, and calculate SGPA (Semester GPA).
- Automatically aggregate SGPA to calculate overall CGPA (Cumulative GPA).

### 4. Internal Assessments
- Keep track of midterms, lab marks, quizzes, and assignments.
- Visualize total scored marks versus maximum possible marks.

### 5. Offline Data & Backups
- The app operates entirely offline using an encrypted local database.
- Easily export all your data (Settings -> Backup & Restore) into a JSON string.
- Import the JSON string on another device to seamlessly transfer your academic profile.

### 6. Personalization
- Follows system Dark/Light mode dynamically.
- Premium Material 3 components with elegant micro-animations.
- Configure global minimum attendance thresholds (e.g., 75%, 80%).
